================================
ALPHA 1.3.0
================================

Additions:
1. Different types of zombies:
    Regular: 20 HP, 5 damage, speed 0.5;
    Runner: 20 HP, 3 damage, speed 1.5;
    Tank: 100-150 HP, 5 damage, speed 0.5, doesn't burn under the Sun;
    Captain: 40-100 HP, 10 damage, speed 1;
2. Different colors for zombies: rainbow colors.
3. New drop table for zombies:
    For all zombies: 50% to drop 1-5 Rotten Flesh;
    Regular: 5% 1-4 Chain, 15% 1-4 Cloth, 5% Fried Egg;
    Runner: 50% 1-4 Cigarette;
    Tank: 50% 1-4 AR15 Bullet;
    Captain: 50% Golden Apple.
4. Armor repair recipes: to repair 2 armor pieces you should arrange them in diagonal pattern in the crafting table.
5. Added Schema Clothes (found in Churches, same durability as Chain Armor).
6. Added Unobtainium.
7. Added Zombie Spawn Egg.
